About Us

Meet the brains behind GEMATacademy.

Why GEMATacademy?

We started GEMATacademy to give everyone a free resource for studying, learning, and appreciating the topics within the course of GEMATMW.

From college professors to your dog, GEMATacademy is open to all. You do not need that much background in Math. Why? Because we do our best to ensure that the topics are understandable and relatable to all.

Join us as we push the appreciation of math to everyone.

A note to the user

We currently only have 3 major topics for discussion. Decision Science, Digital Communication, and Mathematics of Language.

We would like to iterate that this website is purely academic, and is in partial fulfillment of a requirement in GEMATMW under the supervision of Dr. Francis Campena. Though we may present ourselves as such, we are not a professional organization.
